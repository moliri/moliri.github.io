about:


Leesha Maliakal is a recent graduate of the Computer Science program at Northwestern University. She is interested in human computer interaction and artificial intelligence, including areas like tangible interface design, crowdsourcing, social computing, game design, mobile development, natural language processing, and machine learning. For the past year, she has conducted crowdsourcing research under the advisement of Haoqi Zhang at Delta Lab. Her current research explores physical crowdsourcing, specifically, eliciting motivation from an ad-hoc crowd and applying it to a given need.
