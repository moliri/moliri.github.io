Welcome to CrowdCheer!

We are researching how to crowdsource motivational support for runners from spectators in the crowd. CrowdCheer is an iOS app that helps spectators to track and cheer on runners during the race.

What do I get if I participate?

Runners:

  • $30 Amazon gift card
  • $10 bonus for every spectator you recruit that cheers for you using the app (spectator rules below) -- recruit as many supporters as you want, but we will cap the bonus at $50 extra.

Spectators:

  • $30 Amazon gift card

What does this study involve?

Runners:

  • Installing and using CrowdCheer during your race to track your run (much like Runkeeper)
  • Filling out a (5 min) pre-survey now, and a (15 min) post-survey after the race
  • Doing a (20 min) phone/video interview the week after the race at any time that is convenient for you

Spectators:

  • Being out on the race course to cheer during the race
  • Installing and using CrowdCheer to cheer for runners during the race (The app will help you track and cheer for specific runners (just enter their bib #). Periodically, the app will ask you to cheer for other runners at the race. Respond however you like!)
  • Filling out a (5 min) pre-survey now, and a (15 min) post-survey after the race
  • Doing a (20 min) phone/video interview the week after the race at any time that is convenient for you
