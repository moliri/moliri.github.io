Welcome to CrowdCheer!

We are studying how to crowdsource motivational support for runners from spectators in the crowd. CrowdCheer is an iOS app that helps spectators to track and cheer on runners during the race, and helps runners get more support!

What do I get if I participate?

Runners:

  • $30 Amazon gift card
  • $10 bonus for every spectator you recruit that cheers for you using the app (spectator rules below) -- recruit as many supporters as you want, but we will cap the bonus at $50 extra.
  • A free app that helps people track and cheer for you during your race

Spectators:

  • $30 Amazon gift card
  • A free app to help you track and cheer for your runners

What does this study involve?

Runners:

  • Use CrowdCheer app to track your race (like Runkeeper)
  • 5 min pre-survey and 15 min post-survey after the race
  • 20 min phone/video interview at your convenience

Spectators:

  • Be out on the race course to cheer during the race
  • Use CrowdCheer to track and cheer for runners (while you wait for your runners, you may be asked to cheer for others)
  • 5 min pre-survey and 15 min post-survey after the race
  • 20 min phone/video interview at your convenience

1. Complete the 5 min pre-survey now.
