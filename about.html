About Me

This is what I do.

Leesha Maliakal is a Post-Baccalaureate Research Fellow in the EECS department of Northwestern University. She graduated in June 2015 from the Computer Science program at, concentrating in Human-Computer Interaction. For the past year, she has conducted crowdsourcing research under the advisement of Haoqi Zhang at Delta Lab. Her current research explores physical crowdsourcing, specifically, eliciting motivation from an ad-hoc crowd and applying it to a given need. During her career, she hopes to use human-centered design to build innovative technology that will enhance and extend the natural ways in which humans reflect, learn, collaborate, and provide support for one another.
