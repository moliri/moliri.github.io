> welcome :)_

We are thrilled to announce the NEIU Eagle HackFest 2024!

The event will take place on Friday April 12, from 9:00am - 4:00pm on El Centro Campus .

The theme of this year’s hackathon is Better Communities. Students will select from a series of project prompts focused on real-world problems, and will spend the day designing, prototyping, and proposing technology solutions to improve their local communities.

For example:

  • How might we build volunteer systems to help deliver groceries to the elderly?
  • How might we support working parents who want to maintain their work-life balance when switching contexts?
  • How might we help immigrants who want to regularly connect with family in their home countries?
  • How might we help students build and maintain healthy routines when they start college?
  • How might we help first-generation college applicants navigate the college search process?

Join us for a day of food, hacking, mentorship, and learning as we explore how we can improve our communities!

> frequently asked questions (FAQ)_

Who can participate?

College students, 18 years of age and older

Is there a cost to participate?

This is a free event for all participants!

Do I need to have a team to register or attend?

You can participate as an individual. But we encourage you to join a team and hack together. You may plan a team in advance (max 4 students/team), or we will help you form a team the day of the event.

Do I need to know how to code to participate?

No coding experience required! Depending on your experience, you can spend the day prototyping low-fidelity solutions like mock interface sketches, or build out a full web application! It's up to you and your team. We will also have mentors from industry and local universities walking around to support you.

Do I need an idea before coming to the event?

Nope! We will provide teams with problem challenges at the beginning. Then, students will work collaboratively to develop an innovative solution to the problem.

Do I need to bring anything that day?

We recommend you bring a laptop to the event. We will have a few laptops available on site as well. If you will need a laptop, please let us know in your registration form.

Will food be provided at the event?

Yes! The event will have free food.

Can I particpate remotely?

This will be a fully in-person event.

Will there be prizes awarded to top teams?

Yes, top teams will receive prizes at the event! More info to come.
